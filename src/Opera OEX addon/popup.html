SearchenginePreview

Version


Is a extension for Opera which shows thumbnails of websites in the search results of many search engines.

Settings

If you need to change preferences, just open link Preferences.

International versions

If you want to help me translating to other languages please post at my.opera.com in thread SearchenginePreview (en) or send me a email to info@gwendragon.de.
